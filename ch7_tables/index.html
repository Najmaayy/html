Fun Facts About Cats

  1. Cats sleep for about 12–16 hours a day — they are expert nappers!
  2. Cats can make over 100 different sounds.
  3. Cats use their whiskers to measure spaces and detect movement.
  4. A group of cats is called a "clowder."
  5. Some cats are allergic to humans!
