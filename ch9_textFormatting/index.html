Text Formatting

Name Tag Explantion Tag
Emphasis This tag makes words sound more important or stressed when read aloud. It usually looks slanted (italic). Think of it like saying a word with more emotion or meaning. em
Italics This tag tilts the text to the side (like this). It’s often used for titles, foreign words, or to make something stand out a little. i
Underline This tag draws a line under the text. It’s like using a pen to underline something important. u
Bold This tag makes the text look thicker and darker to draw attention — just visual, no extra meaning. b
